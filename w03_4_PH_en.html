Public Health

Since 1999, YPRO has been cooperating with the health care system to create physician order entry system. We have been deeply involved in the public health to understand the huge and complex data flow of the system.

Information System For Diabetes Mellitus Common Care

   Diabetes is a common chronic disease.The prevalence of the diabetes is higher in the world. In addition to taking drugs to control blood sugar, regular blood pressure and blood sugar measurement is the most direct way to grasp the condition of diabetes and self-health management.

   YPRO develop information system for diabetes mellitus common care for the Changhua County Public Health Bureau. This system functions of case notification management and nursing business information, which can shorten the operation process and processing time. It provides a comprehensive public care network for diabetic patients among medical institutions.

Product Picture

Quickly collect case basic, examination, diagnosis, treatment, health education and other data, etc. Establish the national database of diabetes and remaining kidney disease cases.
The statistical chart provided by the system is used to monitor the quality of patient care and statistical analysis of results.
