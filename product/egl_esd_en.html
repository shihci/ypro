Introduction

  The intelligent static tester is used to test whether the static wrist strap and the static foot ring meet the standards of static protection tools, combined with the personnel identification card to generate electronic files and upload them to the server, which is convenient for customers and production departments to manage and view. The machine belongs to precision test instrument.He is required to be calibrated regularly (once a year).

Kit

• ESD testers.
• Stainless steel pedal version (left/right foot separation).
• 12VDC universal power adapter (100-240VAC input power).
•Ground wire.
• Test table and foot pedal connection cable.
•Operation manual.

Features

• Support network communication, upload 60,000 records in less than 14S.
• Realize online upgrade through the network (cross-domain upgrade is possible).
• All parameters are set via PC and can be controlled remotely.
• Comes with a perpetual calendar for accurate timing, and can automatically synchronize the time through the network.
• 60,000 card swiping records, 10 million blacklist capacity, and data is not lost after power failure.
• Abundant I/O ports, which can be used to control other external devices.
• Used by large enterprises, tested by massive data, stable and reliable performance.
